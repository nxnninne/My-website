Introduction

My real name is Punyawee Khanthong, which means brave in Thai. But honestly, it doesn't suit me at all. I’ve never truly lived a brave life. I’m always wearing a mask, never showing my true self or expressing how I really think and feel. I’m just terrified that people will find out who I am—that I’m not strong, I’m not heartless, and I’m not as capable as they think. I’m just a weak person who feels worthless.In a way, my nickname 'Ninja' might fit me better than my real name. I haven't just been deceiving others; I’ve been deceiving myself too. By the time I realized that, it was already too late—so late that I find it hard to even forgive myself

My Goal

My goal is to achieve mastery in both Computer Science and Economics. I don't want one to just supplement the other; I want to integrate them to create a new field of science that the world hasn't fully understood yet. I truly believe that this synergy will lead to a major global transformation.
